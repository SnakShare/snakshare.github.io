SnackShare

Snacking service by snacking enthusiasts

SnackShare was founded on the idea that everyone loves snacks, but not everyone has someone to share snacks with.

Snacking by Snackers

For most snacks, there are no limits to the number of snacks you can share. Certain snacks might have limits set by our snack partners. You will always see the limit in the SnackShare app before sharing.

No more lonely Snacktimes

With SnakChat, you can can snak face to face. With strangers, enemies, and friends! SnakChat comes free with every SnackShare plan!!

Snackers Paradise

Snacks will come in a non-marked package. So you can snack in confidence, without the judgement of your neighbors.
